Come meet Scotty Fulton

Scotty is driven by completion. Completing a task is his measure for success, even if it's a complete failure. He has learned to live by a simple notion:

You can't fail unless you try.

This notion has lead him towards the completion of tasks that he thought would never be attainable for an individual such as himself. This also could be a measure for any other individual who may be seeking encouragement.

Just make sure to have fun while reaching and you'll never be disappointed with the catch.
